It Starts with Us

by Colleen Hoover

★★★★☆

In "It Starts with Us," Colleen Hoover continues the poignant love story of Lily Bloom and Ryle Kincaid, exploring the complexities of love, heartbreak, and second chances. The novel delves into Lily's journey as she navigates the challenges of motherhood, her past relationships, and the emotional impact of her choices.

This sequel to "It Ends with Us" provides a deeper insight into the characters' struggles and growth, showcasing how love can be both beautiful and painful. Hoover's trademark emotional depth shines through as she weaves a tale of resilience and hope amidst turmoil.

Key insights from the book:
- The importance of understanding one's worth and setting boundaries in relationships.
- The significance of healing from past trauma and learning to love again.
- The impact of family dynamics on personal growth and decision-making.
- The power of love in overcoming adversity and finding strength in vulnerability.

"It Starts with Us" is not just a story of romance; it's a testament to the strength of the human spirit, making it a must-read for anyone who believes in the transformative power of love and the importance of starting anew.
